Scope of the Conference

The scope of the Conference includes, but is not necessarily restricted to, advances in the following broad domains:

  • Optical System Design
  • Optical Fabrication Techniques
  • Optical Testing
  • Diffraction Optics
  • Fourier Optics
  • Near Field Optics
  • Astronomical Optics
  • Conformal Optics
  • Biomedical Optics
  • Optical Microscopy
  • Visual Optics
  • Optical Thin Films
  • Optical Interferometry
  • Polarization Optics
  • Optical Sensors
  • Optical Metrology
